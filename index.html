Questions

Things people ask
before downloading.

Is my screen data private?
Completely. Adept captures screenshots on your Mac and everything stays local - nothing is sent to any server, ever. We never see your screen. The only data that leaves your device is what you explicitly choose to share.
How much storage do the screenshots use?
Almost nothing. Adept doesn't store images - it reads the text on your screen using Apple's built-in Vision OCR and saves only the text content. A full week of heavy AI usage takes less than 1 MB. You can also set a retention window (7, 30, 90 days) and Adept auto-deletes older captures.
Will it slow down my Mac?
No. Adept runs at low priority and uses under 1% CPU on average. It automatically slows down its capture rate when your Mac is on battery or the screen is idle - so it stays out of your way.
Can I control what it captures?
Yes. You can blocklist any app from being captured - Adept will skip it entirely. You can also pause recording anytime from the menu bar icon, or stop it completely. You're always in control.
Which AI tools does it support?
Adept installs coaching skills into Claude Code, Cursor, and Gemini. It also detects usage of ChatGPT, Claude.ai, and Perplexity in your browser for reporting purposes. More tools are coming.
What if I want to delete everything?
All your data lives in one folder on your Mac. You can delete it directly from the Adept dashboard, or just trash the folder manually. Uninstalling Adept leaves nothing behind.
Does it require macOS permissions?
Yes - Screen Recording permission, which macOS requires for any app that reads the screen. You grant it once in System Settings and Adept will walk you through it. No other special permissions are needed.
